**A Complete AI Agent Solution**

Agentia offers a robust suite of tools to optimize and streamline your AI development process:

- **Choose Your AI Model**: Flexibility is key, and Agentia supports a wide range of foundational models, including OpenAI models as well as open-source options like Llama3, Anthropic, Mistral, and Gemini Pro. This variety ensures that you can select the ideal model for your specific needs, providing optimal performance for your projects.

- **Enhance Functionality with Tool Integrations**: Integrate seamlessly with Agentia’s toolkit, including voice-calling via Bland.ai, or use your own tools to expand your agent’s capabilities. This allows your agents to perform a wide range of actions and integrate with various systems.

- **Optimize with Prompt Templates**: Maximize your agent's performance with our pre-built prompt templates. Use our prompt improvement system to fine-tune your prompts and ensure seamless communication with your clients.

- **Zero Downtime with Offline Versioning**: Agentia is the only platform that lets you update your AI agents offline, ensuring continuous service with no downtime. Make improvements without disrupting your operations.

- **Train with Your Own Data**: Personalize your agents using your proprietary datasets. Leverage retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to create customized solutions tailored to your clients' needs.

- **Kickstart Development with Templates**: Save time with Agentia’s ready-made templates designed for various industries and use cases. Customize them to fit your requirements and accelerate your development process.
